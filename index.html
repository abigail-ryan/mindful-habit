Why join Mindful Habit

Here at Mindful Habit we believe it's the little moments that make every day special. Too often, we all get caught up in the fast paced life and end up running auto pilot 24/7, all the while missing out on the beauty life has to offer.

Through Mindful Habit's 4 practices

  • Journaling
  • Breathwork
  • Yoga
  • Meditation

We teach you how to show down, breathe and implement these practices to become mindful every day.

Classes

Journaling

Learn to Journal your everyday thoughts and gratitudes, guided by our journal prompts.

Breathwork

Learn to calm the nervous system and be present in the moment with our Breathwork sessions.

Yoga

Join us for evening Yoga Sessions to help reconnect the body and mind after a long days work.

Meditation

Our Meditation Classes teach you how to focus your thoughts and be present in the moment.
